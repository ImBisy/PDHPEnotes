1. Relationships

1.1 Key Qualities

  • Trust: Built through honesty and dependable actions.
  • Respect: Valuing boundaries and opinions without judgment.
  • Empathy: Understanding and validating feelings.
  • Honesty: Clear, truthful communication.
  • Support: Emotional, informational, or practical help.

1.2 Types

  • Family: Foundation of trust and communication.
  • Friendships: Emotional security via shared interests.
  • Romantic: Intimacy requiring boundaries and consent.
  • Professional: Respectful, boundary-driven connections.

2. Power & Communication

2.1 Power Dynamics

  • Balanced: Shared decision-making.
  • Imbalanced: Control, coercion, isolation.

2.2 Communication Skills

  • Active Listening: Reflect to clarify.
  • Assertive: Use “I” statements for needs.
  • Non-Verbal: Align body language and tone.
  • Feedback: Ask questions, give constructive input.

2.3 Conflict Resolution

  1. Identify the issue.
  2. Stay calm—use breaks if needed.
  3. Express via “I” statements.
  4. Brainstorm solutions.
  5. Agree on a plan.
  6. Follow up and adjust.

3. Risk-Taking

3.1 Definition

Exposure to potential harm, measured by likelihood and severity.

3.2 Reasons

  • Thrill-seeking
  • Peer pressure
  • Escape/Coping
  • Self-exploration

4. Drugs

4.1 Definitions

Drug: Substance affecting brain chemistry.

Abuse: Intentional harm or injury to another.

4.2 Classifications & Uses

Type Description Examples Uses
Depressant Slows CNS, reduces arousal Alcohol, Cannabis, Heroin Sleep induction, anxiety relief
Hallucinogen Alters perception & mood LSD, Mushrooms, Ecstasy PTSD, depression treatment
Stimulant Increases alertness & energy Caffeine, Nicotine, Cocaine ADHD meds, recreational

4.3 Effects Comparison

Drug Short-Term Long-Term
Alcohol Slurred speech, poor coordination Liver & brain damage, addiction
Cannabis Red eyes, slowed reactions Memory issues, dependence
Nicotine Increased HR, dizziness Heart disease, lung cancer
Ecstasy Dehydration, mood lift Depression, sleep disturbance
Cocaine Euphoria, high energy Heart issues, addiction
Ice Aggression, rapid breathing Psychosis, brain damage
